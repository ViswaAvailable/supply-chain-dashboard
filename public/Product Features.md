ManufacturingOS is a Demand Planning tool which uses Machine Learning helping Supply Chain leaders and Demand planning teams to accurately plan demand. By combining historical data with "Event-Aware" intelligence, it empowers planners to anticipate demand spikes, manage risks with confidence intervals, and maintain optimal stock levels across all outlets.

## ðŸ“Š Key Modules

### 1. Intelligent Demand Forecasting
*   **Visual Trend Analysis:** Interactive charts visualizing demand against confidence intervals.
*   **Event Correlation:** "Event Bands" overlay on charts to instantly correlate demand spikes with specific promotions or holidays.
*   **Granular Breakdown:** Daily forecast tables with sorting, filtering (SKU, Category, Outlet), and dedicated event visibility.
*   **Risk Management:** Clear visualization of "Lower" and "Upper" confidence bounds to aid in safety stock planning.

### 2. Advanced Event Manager
*   **Proactive Planning:** Define holidays, promotions, and custom events before they happen.
*   **Smart Scoping:** Apply events globally or target specific **Outlets**, **Categories**, or individual **SKUs**.
*   **Uplift Modeling:** Define expected "Uplift %" to automatically adjust forecasts for the duration of the event.
*   **Status Control:** Toggle events on/off to simulate scenarios without deleting data.

### 3. SKU-Level Control Center
*   **Human-in-the-Loop Overrides:** Set "Minimum Quantity" floors that override algorithm outputs to ensure critical stock availability.
*   **Categorization:** Manage and organize SKUs by category (e.g., Biscuits, Snacks, Beverages) for better reporting.
*   **Search & Filter:** deeply searchable dropdowns for managing large catalogs efficiently.
